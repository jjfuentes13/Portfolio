Bellum Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

An Attendance App

In response to the challenges a local BJJ gym faces in tracking and promoting students, I designed and developed a custom attendance app, the Bellum Gym Attendance App. The app streamlines the process of tracking student attendance by allowing parents and instructors to check in their children or students for each class easily. The app improves the efficiency of tracking attendance and assists the gym owner and head instructor in monitoring progress and determining when to promote students based on the number of classes attended. Throughout the development process, I collaborated closely with the gym owner and their staff, gathering feedback and iterating on the app's design to create a user-friendly and effective solution. The Bellum Gym Attendance App is now available on the Google Play Store.
